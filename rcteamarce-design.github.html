Capital One Volunteers at the Dog Shelter

Our Mission

We believe every dog deserves unconditional love and care. Capital One volunteers dedicate their time and hearts to providing shelter dogs with comfort, companionship, and hope for a brighter future. Together, we're not just changing dogs' lives— we're transforming our community.

Meet Our Leaders

🦮
Ignacio Rodriguez
Program Director

Ignacio leads our volunteer initiative with passion and dedication. His vision is to create a community where every dog receives the love and attention they deserve. With years of experience in animal welfare, Ignacio ensures that our shelter provides the highest quality of care.

🐶
Christofer Arce
Community Coordinator

Christofer connects volunteers with the dogs that need them most. His enthusiasm and organizational skills ensure that every volunteer has a meaningful experience. Christofer believes that the bond between humans and dogs can change the world.

The Impact We Make

❤️

Love & Companionship

Every dog gets daily attention, playtime, and affection from our dedicated volunteers who treat them like family.

🏥

Health & Wellness

We ensure proper nutrition, exercise, and medical care, preparing each dog for a healthy, happy life ahead.

🏠

Hope for a Home

Well-trained, socialized, and loved dogs have better chances of finding their forever families.

🤝

Community Growth

Our volunteers grow too—building friendships, gaining skills, and finding purpose in giving back.
